lingvo.tasks.asr.decoder_metrics.BeamSearchDecodeOutputToDecoderTopK(decoder_outs, *, ids_to_strings_fn, tag='')[source]¶

Converts BeamSearchDecodeOutput to DecoderTopK.

As a side-effect, also creates TF nodes used by eval pipelines (“top_k_decoded” and “top_k_scores”).

Parameters

  • decoder_outs – a beam_search_helper.BeamSearchDecodeOutput instance.

  • ids_to_strings_fn – a function of (ids, lens) -> strings, where ids has shape [batch, length], lens has shape [batch], and strings has shape [batch].

  • tag – optional tag for tf.identity() names.

Returns

A DecoderTopK instance.
